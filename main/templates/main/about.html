What's this?

This integration is run by Bastian Greshake Tzovaras. Once set-up, this app will automatically keep an up-to-date copy of your Last.fm scrobbles in your Open Humans account.

How will you use my data?

When you log in, this app will only store your access tokens from Open Humans and the Last.fm username you enter.

It will process your Last.fm data but not store any of this raw data in this application. Instead, the data is only sent to Open Humans where it will stored long-term and where you can delete the data at any time.
